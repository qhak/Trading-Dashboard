Cluster Trading Dashboard

More compact layout, cleaner checklist rows, and manual pair adding. Each card is smaller so you can scan more pairs at once and still jump to your own TradingView chart.

I changed the chart area to a simpler compact TradingView embed with the toolbar hidden, because the old one was probably what felt “weird”. Your own drawn charts still won’t show inside plain HTML, so use the chart link button to open your personal TradingView layout.
Tip: if you want to scan even faster, keep only your active pairs in the dashboard and use the chart link button for deep review.
